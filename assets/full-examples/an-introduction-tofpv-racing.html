Essential Hardware Components

Anatomy of a Racing Drone

Frame
Carbon fiber X-structure. Rigid, lightweight, and durable for crashes.
Vision
FPV Camera & VTX. Provides real-time analog or digital video feed.
Power
Brushless Motors & ESCs. High RPM output for immense thrust.
Brain
Flight Controller (FC). Processes gyro data to stabilize flight.

Flight Dynamics

Basic Mechanics of Flight

Understanding the 4 axes of control

Pitch

Tilting nose up or down for forward/backward movement.

Roll

Tilting left or right for lateral movement.

Yaw

Rotating on the vertical axis to change heading.

Throttle

Controls motor power and total altitude.

Acro / Rate Mode

Unlocks full acrobatic potential by removing self-leveling. The pilot must manually correct every movement.

Rules of the Air

Safety & Responsibility

Visual Observer (VO)

Always fly with a spotter to maintain situational awareness while you are under the goggles.

Airspace Authorization

Use LAANC apps (e.g., Aloft, AirMap) to check for restricted zones and get instant approval.

Community Guidelines

Follow CBO safety rules (AMA or FPV Freedom Coalition) to remain compliant with regulations.
